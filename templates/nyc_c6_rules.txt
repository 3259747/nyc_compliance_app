NEW YORK CITY ZONING RESOLUTION - C6 DISTRICT COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK
SECTION 33-43: SKY EXPOSURE PLANE AND TOWER REGULATIONS
High-density commercial development parameters specify a maximum permissible Floor Area Ratio (FAR) of 15.00 for office, commercial, and mixed-use transit hub structures. Maximum building height is governed by dynamic tower coverage allowances up to 100% block frontages. Minimum rear yard depth is explicitly mandated at 20 feet min for commercial parcels. Loading berths are strictly verified based on gross programmatic floor area.
